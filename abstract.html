ABSTRACT

This community service project aims to identify and promote comprehensive daily checkups and wellness practices for women. It focuses on raising awareness about routine health assessments, including physical and reproductive well-being. Through educational workshops, medical screenings, and collaborations with healthcare professionals, the project seeks to enhance early detection, preventive care, and holistic wellness. By providing accessible healthcare resources, it empowers women to take charge of their health, fostering long-term well-being in the community.
